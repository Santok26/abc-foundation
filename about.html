About ABC Foundation

Our Story

ABC Foundation was founded in 2010 with a mission to protect and preserve our environment. Over the years, we have grown from a small group of passionate individuals to a leading NGO making significant impacts in water conservation, tree plantation, waste management, and community education. Our journey began with a simple idea: that every person can make a difference, and together, we can create a sustainable future for generations to come.

Vision and Mission

Vision: A world where humans live in harmony with nature, ensuring a healthy planet for all living beings.

Mission: To implement sustainable environmental solutions through community engagement, education, and innovative projects that protect and restore our natural resources.
